**Name**: Logging Level  
**Type**: String  
**Default**: 1   
**Available In**: Revolution 2.2.0+

The default logging level; the lower the level, the fewer messages that are logged. 

Available options: 0 (FATAL), 1 (ERROR), 2 (WARN), 3 (INFO), and 4 (DEBUG).
